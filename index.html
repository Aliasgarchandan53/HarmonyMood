About Us


HarmonyMood stems from the belief that music is a universal language that resonates with our emotions. Our mission is to enhance the emotional connection between users and their music choices through AI-powered sentiment analysis, curating personalized music recommendations that touch the soul.

We envision a world where music becomes an exquisite expression of feelings, a constant source of inspiration. Join us on this journey of emotions and melodies as we redefine the way we experience music.
